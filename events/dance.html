hit the bell
Natraj Solo (Narthanam)

Rules and Regulations:

  1. PRELIMINARY ROUND (online)
    • Time limit: 2-3 minutes
    • No video editing is allowed.
    • 3 entries per college will be accepted.
    • Last date for submission: 11th February 2023.
  2. FINAL ROUND (offline)
    • Time limit: 5-7 minutes (including stage setup)
    • The song should be purely classical. Semi-classical or Bollywood songs are strictly not allowed.
    • Participants are requested to bring your own college ID cards.
    • Kindly bring your pre-recorded music in mp3 format in a pen drive.
    • No props shall be provided by the college.
    • The use of flames, powder, and fluids are strictly prohibited on stage.
    • The decisions of the judges will be final.

Kindly note: This competition is open for only those students who are pursuing bachelor's degree. Post graduate students are not allowed to participate in the competition.

kick the bell
The Online Solo Indian Classical Dance Competition

Rules and Regulations:

  1. Participants are requested to submit their performance video at natrajpgdav1@gmail.com after registering.
  2. The video should be recorded from one angle. Editing the video or usage of any filters is strictly prohibited.
  3. Time limit for the video is 5 to 7 minutes including the narration time.
  4. The performance video should be in proper costume according to the dance form.
  5. The song should be purely classical. Semi- classical or bollywood songs are strictly not allowed.
  6. The decision of the judges shall be final.
  7. Prelims: 10-15 participants will be selected and will be judged in the final round by the Judges. (Prelims result will be out on 15th April'21 on our social media handles. Final round will be on 17th April'21 on zoom/meet)

Kindly note: This competition is open for only those students who are pursuing bachelor's degree. Post graduate students are not allowed to participate in the competition.
