THE ANNUAL

CULTURAL FEST

Aaghaz is the Annual Cultural Fest of PGDAV College and is organized with great pomp and show. It's extended over in two days, where each day inculcates numerous competitions of our adept college societies. This year the 5th and 6th of April, 2022 will witness the fall of Aaghaz.
